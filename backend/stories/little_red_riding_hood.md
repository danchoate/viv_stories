# Little Red Riding Hood

“Hi, my name's Wolf. Everybody thinks I am the bad guy in Little Red Riding 
Hood. But let me tell you the real story”.  When I woke up I had pancakes 
with syrup for breakfast. It was just the start of a normal day. Then I 
started walking to my job. I am a lumber jack. I cut wood for a living. 
That’s right a lot of people think that animals can’t be lumber jacks. But 
they are wrong. 

I was walking to my job. When this girl in a red cloak bumped into me. Then 
she started bragging about how awesome her Grandma is. On and on. A moment 
later I said, “Where are you going?”. After that she started bragging about 
her Grandma. And this time about her Grandma's house. Like all the fun games 
her Grandma has. She kinda made me feel left out. Because my Grandma Jane 
died a week ago. I miss her a lot. Now all I can hear from this girl's mouth 
is blah blah this blah blah that. Then I said “where does she live”. To be 
nice and to get a job. She said “ 50, Mulberry lane” 

Afterward I said “Why don’t you pick her some flowers, maybe the daisies 
right behind you”. To be nice. If you know what I mean. She said “Fine”! In 
a sarcastic tone. Shortly after that,  she picked some flowers and skipped 
off.  Then I thought why don’t I go to Grandma's house to get a job. I need 
money! Because I am getting no jobs because I am a Wolf. When I got there I 
knocked on the door and I asked. “Do you need a lumber jack?”Then she said 
“come on in”. Then she said “do you like hot chocolate sweetie”. “Sure”. When 
she was going to get the hot chocolate she got locked in the kitchen. So 
then I thought. I wanted the girl to have a Grandma. She was so excited 
about it. So I dressed up as her Grandma. And when I heard the doorbell. 
I jumped in bed!

When she opened the door. Then she closed the door and sat next to the bed. 
Soon, she started telling me about her walk here. And the scary wolf she saw
and the daisies she found ,and how she lost the treats she was going to give 
to Grandma on the way. Then I said “ sweetie pie thank you for coming all 
this way for me”. Then she started asking weird questions. Like “ What big 
teeth you have Grandma”. I said “All the better to eat with”. Then little 
red got scared of  me and ran away. Then I used my chainsaw to get grandma 
out of the kitchen. She paid me $5.00. I wasn’t the real bad guy, I was just 
having a normal day. But now it's on the news and I lost my job because some 
little girl ran away because she thought I was scary. But I was just being 
myself, my nice caring self. Now I am trying to find a new job. 

That's the real story of Little Red Riding Hood (The bragger)
