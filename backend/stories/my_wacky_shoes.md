# My wacky shoes 

I thought it was going to be a normal Sunday but boy was I wrong.”Beep beep” 
is how my phone woke me up that morning.It was my mail, it said new shoes 
out, ugg slippers, but  the store opens at 8:00 sharp and there are only 
100 in the world and 5 at my mall today. When I heard that, I ran to my blue 
jeep, drove to the mall, and then ran inside. A few seconds later I saw the 
last pair of new uggs standing right in front of me. I pushed and shoved 
people to get to the pair but it was too late, they were gone. I started to 
walk away, but then I looked down under the display and there was a pair!! I 
picked it up  and ran to the checkout counter. People were chasing me for 
those shoes! 

When I was all checked out, I walked to my car and drove home. My parents 
weren't awake, so I took my dog Sandy, a golden retriever, for a short walk. 
Before we went outside I slid my new uggs on . They are so different from 
OG Uggs. They have a blue bottom that gets lighter as the color moves up the 
shoe. I got outside and went on my usual walk with Sandy. We went to the dog 
park and Sandy played with her friends. Then on the walk home, my new shoes 
magically started jumping and got me into a pickle! I was in the middle of 
the highway. I saw red cars, blue cars, green cars, and even gold and shiny 
cars. I struggled to walk. Then I realized I was on the L.A highway . How did 
my shoes get me here? I wondered. What's next! Then poof my shoes took me 
into a stranger's car. It was not the prettiest car; it had grey seats and it 
was full of trash. Then poof all the way to Maine, I saw a ski hill and a 
pool table. Then poof all the way to Asia. I was there for like 5 minutes 
and I saw a normal panda and then a clumsy one. Then poof to Times Square in 
New York. I saw so many screens and ads. Then poof to a barn with a million 
goats, one only had one leg. I felt sorry for him. Then back to the highway 
I started at. What a day!

I tried to get my shoes off but they wound’t budge. Then I started to think 
about the stature of liberty and then poof the next thing i’m there. Next i 
think of my bedroom then poof i am sitting at my desk. Next I think about my 
career when I grow up and poof my head takes me to the future. I am a lead 
singer in a band. Oh I get it now! Wherever I think of, my shoes take me 
there or show me what will happen. So I will just think of being out on the 
road with my dog without these shoes. poof. I am in a flower bed infrount 
of my neighbors house. Walking with my Sandy. “No sandy don’t chase that 
poor innocent squirrel”.” Bark bark” sandy barks. 

Then when I get home I find my shoes in my closet. I will save that for 
another day. I want an adventure! When my parents woke up we had breakfast! 
I hope I remember this weird moment. I will know not to get the new pair of 
uggs or shoes unless i know they will not get me into a puzzle again like 
that. What a sunday!
