# The Mysterious Christmas by Vivienne Choate 

## Introduction 

Once upon a time there was a lovely family, the Tomsons to be exact. 
Caroline Tomson (that’s me!), Fred Tomson, Jess Tomson, Tod Tomson, a 
loving father and Jeffrey Basous, my uncle. You're probably wondering how I 
am related to Jeffrey Basous. Because my older sister Sadie is married to him . 

“Sweetheart, stop writing your novel and come downstairs, your friend 
is here” my mom said. 

“My bestie Zestie Caroline,” yelled Sydney up the stairs. 

“Sydney,” I yelled. My best friend's name is Sydney if you were wondering who 
in the world is Sydney.

“Let's go out and play in the snow,” said Sydney 

“Sure,” I said. 

We frolicked in the snow, we made snow angels and last but not least we 
made a really tall, really big snow man. He has the same name as my dad, 
Tod. I just really like that name, that's all. 

When Sydney's mom came I burst into tears because I did not want her to leave.

Emma is her mom’s name. 

“Emma, can she please stay for Christmas? You can come in the morning to 
pick her up. She can sleep in my bed please," I said. 

“Yea, mom, please,” Sydney said.

“No, we have to wrap presents, go to your ice hockey game and then go to 
your brother's football game and then it would be ten p.m so I don’t think 
so but we can plan one for later though if you want. Where’s your mom 
Caroline?” Said Emma a.k.a Sydney’s mom.

“Hi Emma, we have to wrap some presents too. Would you like to come in 
for a quick cup of tea?,” My mom said 

“Sorry I’ve got to run but maybe another time this year.I will text you 
about it later. Bye.” 

“Mom! Where are the Christmas trees and presents?!,” I screamed.

“Oh no, is the mini Christmas tree gone too?” My mom said 

“Yes mom, I said Christmas trees not Christmas tree,”.  I said  

“Let's go look for them now,” I said. 

“Sure, let's do it because if we don’t find them before midnight then we 
won’t have Christmas. Sorry, sweetie.”

We looked high and low and in between and there was no sight of our Christmas 
trees or presents.

“I really hope we find them,” I said 

When my dad got home I told him about it and he screamed and so did my 
brother, like dad like son. 

My brother said “you're probably blind and that's why you don’t see them,” to 
my whole family, but when he walked in the living room he said “no no no no.” 

In my notebook which I write in to help me solve crimes I wrote:

Suspect one, my uncle, yes my uncle. First, he lives 30 minutes away and he 
said he was getting a Christmas tree when we saw him a week ago and when we 
called him today he had the same Christmas tree we had and same decor so 
thats why i suspect him .

Suspect two, our next door neighbors, because yesterday they didn't have a 
Christmas tree but now they do and  it has my face on it because the tree 
had an ornament of me so that's why I think it’s them. 

Suspect three, my dad. He has been very quiet and he is only quiet when he 
has done something he doesn't want us to hear about. He has been very funny 
too and he is only funny when he has done something he doesn't want  us to 
know  or he could have got everything soaked when he tried to water the 
tree and took it outside and forgotten about it.

So I am going to break this case. I biked to my uncle's house. I looked in 
his room then his living room but there was nothing to be seen. He also gave 
me peanut butter and jelly for the road. 

When I was at the next suspect's house, the next door neighbors, I noticed 
something fishy, like actually I saw a fish. I looked around.

I asked if I could go in their basement but they said no.

So I really think it was the next door neighbor but how can I get in 
their basement? 

I thought for hours and hours but I couldn't think of a plan. Then at 
11:00 pm I got it. I have 1 hour left to find it. 

This is my plan. I wrote it in my notebook. 

So in one hour I will break one window and tip toe downstairs through their 
basement door. But it's not that easy. Their basement door is locked and I do 
not have the key. So I will get their backup key which they keep in their 
room on their desk and race downstairs and find our tree and presents. Now 
I know why their last name is Grinch because they are Grinches. Let's go!! 

When I got there I broke the window nearest to their bedroom door. I tiptoed 
into their bedroom. “Where is the key,” I thought to myself. Then I saw a 
shiny light in their bed. It was in Mr. Grinch’s hands. I was so scared. I 
grabbed it then I ran downstairs to find our trees and presents

Then I saw my lovely tree and brought it home with the presents. 

When my family woke up they were bursting with joy!!!!! Then when they 
opened their presents from me they were even more excited. I got my brother 
the new skateboard he wanted, I got my dad this new shoe brand and cook 
book he wanted. For my mom, I got her a spa certificate and a spa set she can 
use at home. My brother got the skateboard I got him, robux, a new dog, a 3D 
printer and glasses that are fake but make him look smart. He also got a new 
mini Christmas tree. It’s as teeny weeny as your pinky. I got a new dog (me 
and my brother always get our own dogs) and the newest surfboard because I 
love surfing. I also got “The Money Machine” which was supposed to give you 
as much money as you want but all it gave me was one jolly rancher, a 
hairball and the smallest donut in the world. I also got something really 
expensive, an iPhone 18.

I really hope I have a cool crime I have to solve again. I will always 
remember my great Christmas and the great presents. I will always remember 
to guard my Christmas tree on Christmas Eve just in case. My heart is 
leaping with joy and I have a big smile on my face because I’m happy I 
saved Christmas.
